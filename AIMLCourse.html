Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML) represent the fusion of human-like intelligence with the vast computational power of machines, enabling systems to learn from data, adapt over time, and make informed decisions. It’s the art of teaching machines to think, reason, and evolve, unlocking endless possibilities to solve complex problems, enhance human capabilities, and shape a smarter, more connected world.
